Frequently Asked Questions

Is PowerGuide available outside of Dagupan City? ▼
Currently, our real-time monitoring and crowdsourced reporting are optimized for Dagupan City and surrounding local municipalities. We plan to expand to the rest of Pangasinan soon.
Does the battery tracker work offline? ▼
Yes! The Smart Battery Tracking feature uses local device APIs to monitor levels without needing an internet connection. However, notification syncing requires a brief connection.
