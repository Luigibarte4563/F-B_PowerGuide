Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 6, 2026

Introduction

At POWERGUIDE, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our community members in Dagupan City and beyond. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our outage reporting and battery tracking services.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data: To provide accurate real-time monitoring, we collect approximate location data when you report an outage.

Device Information: Our Smart Battery Tracker monitors device battery percentage and health locally to provide alerts.

Account Info: Email addresses provided during sign-up or subscription are used solely for alerts and security updates.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data helps us build the Crowdsourced Outage Map. Individual reports are aggregated to show neighborhood-level status while keeping your specific house number private.

We do NOT sell your data to third-party advertisers. We use it only to improve grid reliability information for the community.

3. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your account information at any time through the dashboard. For data export requests, please contact our support team.
